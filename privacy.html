Northampton Symphony Orchestra

Privacy Policy

Northampton Symphony Orchestra has created this policy because we want you to feel confident about the privacy and security of your personal information.

Use of Personal Information
The contact details you supply (name, address, telephone number and email address) will only be used to provide you with the information you have requested. If you become a Friend of NSO your details will be added to our Friends mailing list so we can keep you informed of future concerts and will be retained for as long as you remain a Friend of NSO. If you reserve tickets for NSO concerts through this web site we will only retain your information until the date of the concert, in case we need to contact you regarding any late changes or cancellations.

Disclosure of Information
Your personal details will only be used by Northampton Symphony Orchestra and we will NOT disclose this information to anyone else without first obtaining your permission.

Email Addresses
Northampton Symphony Orchestra will not send you unwanted email messages or junk mail, nor will we pass your email address to any third party engaged in such activities. If you subscribe to our mailing list you can unsubscribe at any time.

Use of Cookies
A cookie is a small file which some web sites create and store on your computer to identify if you have visited the site previously. In some cases cookies are used to track which pages you have viewed or to provide personalised content such as welcoming you by name if you have supplied this information in a previous visit. Northampton Symphony Orchestra does not use cookies on the NSO web site and therefore no information on your web browsing habits is collected or stored either on your computer or ours.

On-line Ticket Purchases
All transactions for tickets bought through the NSO website are carried out by We Got Tickets who have their own privacy policy which can be found at the foot of their main page. If you wish buy tickets using their service we recommend you read their privacy policy beforehand. Northampton Symphony Orchestra does not have access to any of your financial information as a result of on-line ticket purchases made through our website.

Supporting NSO
On-line purchases made by following links from this website are provided by individual retailers who each have their own privacy policy, which can usually be found at the foot of their main page. If you wish to make on-line purchases to support NSO we recommend you read the retailer's privacy policy beforehand. Northampton Symphony Orchestra does not have access to any of your personal information, order history or billing data as a result of any purchase you make through the NSO website.

Third Party Sites
We provide carefully selected links to other web sites that may be of interest to you. Northampton Symphony Orchestra is in no way responsible for the content or privacy practices of these web sites, and our Privacy Policy does not apply to any site other than the NSO web site. While we will not knowingly link to sites that intentionally gather personal information for inappropriate purposes, we would recommend you check how other web sites handle such data before giving them any personal information.

If you have any questions concerning the way we use your personal information, please contact the webmaster by email.
