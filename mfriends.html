Friends Scheme

Northampton Symphony Orchestra re-launched its Friends Scheme during the 2002 ~ 2003 Season to help strengthen the links with our audience. For many in the orchestra the highlight of each season is the exclusive Friends' Summer Concert and Buffet, which provides an opportunity to meet and thank in person many of the people whose generous support helps make the Orchestra such a success. Everyone who is able to attend thoroughly enjoys the event.


Friends of NSO receive the following benefits:

  • Discounted ticket prices 
  • Free programme for each NSO concert 
  • Priority booking
  • Two free tickets to the Friends' exclusive Summer Concert and Buffet
  • Regular newsletters
  • Invitations to open rehearsals
Becoming a Friend costs just £20.00 per season, and your membership fee is recovered by joining the orchestra for the exclusive concert and buffet - the discount on concert tickets represents an additional saving. You only need to purchase tickets for the concerts you are able to attend, rather than subscribing to the entire season. Joining the Friends Scheme helps the orchestra forward plan, thus helping to ensure the future success of one of the finest community orchestras in the country.


If you would like to join the Friends Scheme, or wish to renew your membership, please download, print off and complete the following form send it to us with a cheque for £20.00 (payable to NSO) and let us do the rest!
