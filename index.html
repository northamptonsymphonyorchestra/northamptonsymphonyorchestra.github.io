Our Next Concert: Malcom Arnold at 100

The Northampton Symphony Orchestra is a large community orchestra with over 125 years of history and a high standard of performance.

Our next concert: Malcolm Arnold and Camille Saint-Saens

Purchase Tickets for Our Next Concert

We are delighted to announce our first concert of the 2021/22 season. Join us in celebrating Northampton's own Sir Malcolm Arnold on what would have been his 100th birthday

We will be performing two pieces by the famous composer- a Great, Great Overture (with rifles, hoovers, and a floor polisher in the percussion section!) and the exciting Sympony no 2. We will close the programme with a perfomance of Saint Saens majestic and epic Organ Symphony

The concert will take place at Saint Matthews Church in Northampton at 7:30 pm on October 23rd- you can purchase tickets using the secure button above. In the meantime, keep safe and happy and we look forward to seeing you there.
