NSO 2019/20 - A Season of Contrasts - The Malcolm Arnold Festival

In the first concert of our 126th season, we are pleased to join the annual Malcolm Arnold Festival with a selection from Sir Malcolm Arnold's prolific catalog of works, including a film music suite, a historical overture , and a set of variations on themes by his good friend (and excellent composer) Ruth Gipps. We will be joined by award winning soloist Rythmie Wong for Rachmaninoff’s incredible Piano Concerto no. 2 and round out the programme with Tchaikovsky’s ever popular concert overture from Romeo and Juliet. You can use the link below to find out more, or scroll down to buy your tickets now. The concert will take place 12th October at Saint Matthew’s Church in Northampton at 7:30 pm.
