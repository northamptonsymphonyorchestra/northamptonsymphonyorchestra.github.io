A Word from the Committee of the NSO

After much discussion about the health and safety of our musicians and audience we have taken the decision to cancel the remaining concerts in our 2019 – 2020 season. This is a decision we have not taken lightly, and we remain committed to bringing our Sixth Sense and American Contrasts concert programmes to you in the 2021 -2022 season.

In the meantime, we are very pleased to announce our 2020 -2021 season, Music She Wrote- a season celebrating women in music. Each concert will feature at least one piece composed by a woman. In the online brochure below you can see the concerts we have planned, along with a few links to Youtube performances of some of the pieces. We are all very excited to bring these fabulous pieces to you along with standards of the repertoire by Tchaikovksy, Shostakovitch, Wagner and many others.

To each of you viewing this page, please stay safe and healthy. We look forward to seeing you again soon.
