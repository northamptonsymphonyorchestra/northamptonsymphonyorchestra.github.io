A Symphonic Gallery - Forty Years of Star Wars

Join John Gibbons and the Northampton Symphony Orchestra on 24th of February, 7:30 pm at Christ Church for an exploration of music from film. From Bernard Herrmann's spine tingling score to "Psycho" - itself inspired by artist Edward Hopper's House on a Hill- to a selection of fantastic film scores by British composers- to John Williams's iconic and Academy award nominated score for "Star Wars: The Force Awakens" our Symphonic Gallery of film music promises to be a thrilling and enjoyable evening.
