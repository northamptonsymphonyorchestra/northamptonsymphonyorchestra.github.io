A Symphonic Gallery - The Great Wave off Kanagawa

Please john John Gibbons and the Northampton Symphony Orchestra in the next instalment of our Symphonic Gallery. This time, we will be exploring music inspired by the ocean, with Debussy’s incredible masterpiece La Mer (itself inspired by Katsushika Hokusai’s Great Wave off Kanagawa), Rimsky-Korsakov’s music from Sadko, Bax’s exploration of the Garden of Fand, and Peaslee’s fantastic trombone concerto, Arrows of time. We are joined by one of the world’s finest female trombonists, Carol Jarvis. The concert will be held April 21st 2018 at 7:30 at Christ Church.
