NSO’s 125th Season – A Victorian Christmas

The NSO looks back on its Victorian beginnings with a concert of music composed during, and inspired by, the Victorian era. We will be joined by compere and narrator Allan Bell. Featuring a selection from Tchaikovsky's "The Nutcracker", Humperdinck's Hansel and Gretel, Bryan Kelly's Scrooge, Lionel Bart's Selections from Oliver and many more of your festive favourites!
