NSO’s 125th Season – FANFARE!

Join us for the first concert of our 125th Season on 10th November 2018, 7:30 pm, at Saint Michael’s Church for Aaron Copland’s monumental and uplifting Third Symphony – which the composer based on his famous “Fanfare for the Common Man”. Dynamic cellist Yaraslava Trofymchuck joins the orchestra for Dvorak’s famous Cello Concerto, and we open the concert in grand style with Johan Halvorsen’s Entry March of the Boyars.
