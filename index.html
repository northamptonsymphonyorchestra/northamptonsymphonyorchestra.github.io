Conductor: John Gibbons
Brahms Academic Festival Overture
Strauss Horn Concerto No.1

Tchaikovsky Symphony No.4

Tickets

Adults Concessions* Children under 16
In advance £12.00 £10.00 2 free with a paying adult/concession; otherwise £6.00
On the door £13.00 £11.00 2 free with a paying adult/concession; otherwise £7.00
*Concessions: over 60, unemployed, disabled and full-time students

Tickets available from NSO Ticket Secretary

Telephone: 01604 792207

Please leave a contact telephone number together with how many tickets you wish to purchase and the NSO Ticket Secretary will contact you with payment details. 

Alternatively, please complete this form with your ticket requirements, stating clearly whether you wish to pay in advance or reserve and pay on the door.

Tickets can be purchased on the door using cash or cheque, but please note we cannot accept credit/debit cards at the concert venue. Reserved tickets will be held for collection and payment at the venue until 15 minutes before the concert starts, after which time any unclaimed tickets will be offered for general sale.
