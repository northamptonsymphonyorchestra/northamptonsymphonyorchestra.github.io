EVENT POSTPONED

28th March 2020

Due to concerns for the health and safety of our musicians and audience, we regretfully announce the postponement of our Sixth Sense concert. If you have purchased a ticket online we will process a refund for you. It is our sincere hope to be able to present this beautiful music with you later this year. In the meantime, please stay safe.
