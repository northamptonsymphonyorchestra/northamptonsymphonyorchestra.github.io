NSO at Clifton Cathedral

For the first time ever, the NSO is on tour with one fantastic concert as part of the Cilfton International Festival of Music at Clifton Cathedral in Bristol. We will be performing Doreen Carwithen’s The Men of Sherwood Forest Overture, Tchaikovsky’s Romeo and Juliet Fantasy, and (joined by virtuoso pianist Arta Arnicane) the first William Alwyn Piano Concerto and Rachmaninoff’s ever popular Second Piano Concerto.

The concert is on the 16th of November at 7:45 pm at Clifton Cathedral, Bristol. Please note- this concert is not part of the orchestra’s 2019/20 season and therefore Friends’ discounts do not apply. Funded in part by the generosity of the William Alwyn Foundation.

To book tickets please click here or visit our “Concerts” page.

NSO 2019/20 - A Season of Contrasts - A Christmas Cracker

In our ever popular Christmas Cracker, this year we will be embarking on an exciting adventure into the world of Heroes and Villains. Join us for music from Frozen, The Grinch Who Stole Christmas, and Superman, as well as Carl Nielsen's ALADDIN, narrated by compere extraordinaire ALAN BELL- along with lots of your Christmas favourites, carols, mince pies and mulled wine. Come dressed as your favourite hero or villain!

You can use the link below to find out more, or scroll down to buy your tickets now. The concert will take place 8th December at Spinney Theatre in Northampton at 3:00 pm.
