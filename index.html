Christmas in the Toy Box

3.00pm Sunday 13th December 2015
Spinney Theatre, Northampton. NN3 6DG [map]

Buy Tickets

Conductor: John Gibbon
Leader: Trevor Dyson

 

Programme to include...

Jessel: Parade of the Tin Soldiers
Chappell: Paddington Bear's First Concert
Newman: Toy Story Suite
Arnold: Fantasy on Christmas Carols
Delius: Sleigh Ride
A selection of carols
...plus complementary mulled wine and mince pies!
Tickets:
  • Adults: £15
  • Concessions: £13
  • Children: £1 each for the first two or £7 thereafter.
