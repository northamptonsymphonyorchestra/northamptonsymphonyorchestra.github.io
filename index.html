Our Next Concert: A Christmas Movie Cracker

The Northampton Symphony Orchestra is a large community orchestra with over 125 years of history and a high standard of performance.

Book CHRISTMAS CRACKER tickets now

Join the Northampton Symphony Orchestra and compere Alan Bell for our Christmas Movie Cracker. Please do book tickets early to avoid disappointment for this very popular concert. As always, keep safe and happy and we look forward to seeing you there.
