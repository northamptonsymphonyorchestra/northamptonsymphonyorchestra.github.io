Our Next Concert: Nielsen, Shostakovich, Rachmaninoff

The Northampton Symphony Orchestra is a large community orchestra with over 125 years of history and a high standard of performance.

12th March 2022 - 7:30 pm - St. Matthew's Church, The Drive, Kingsley, Northampton NN1 4RY

GET YOUR TICKETS NOW

Join us for the first concert of the new year with Musical Director John Gibbons, leader Emily Groom, and internationally recognised piano soloist Maria Marchant for a fantastic mostly Russian concert. We will be performing Rachmaninoff’s intensely dramatic second symphony, Nielson’s celebration of the sunrise in the Helios Overture, and Shostakovich’s gloriously free and wistful Piano Concerto no 2.

We have had sold out all previous performances thus far this season, so we recommend you reserve your tickets early for this popular programme.
