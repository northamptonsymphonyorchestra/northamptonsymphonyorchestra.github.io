Members

If you would like to join the orchestra please fill in this form and our membership secretary will be in touch.

The current rehearsal calendar can be found below, or you can download a PDF version using the button. If you're going to miss a rehearsal or performance please email Angie directly.

Below the calendar are three resources: your Member's brochure which contains programmes, orchestrations, and links to audio or video performances of all pieces on this year's programme, then a link to download the 2018/19 Season Brochure - Please feel free to distribute this to your family, friends and colleagues, and lastly, a spotify playlist of the selections on this programme.
