2017/18 - A Symphonic Gallery

Our 124th season is an exploration of the relationship between the visual arts and orchestral music, with at least one piece on each concert inspired by painting, sculpture, or filmmaking.

The online brochure below is a way of finding out more about the music and art we will be sharing this season - click on the image for a concert schedule; interesting content about the music, art, and soloists; and links to book your tickets for each concert, or scroll down for a quick view of our concerts and programmes.
