We are Back!

We are excited to share the details of our season. From Star Wars to Shostakovich, there is something for everyone in every concert. As always, the NSO endeavours to present programmes as they are advertised. Should events transpire that require the orchestra to cancel a concert, we will issue full refunds for any advance tickets. Similarly, should restrictions be placed on the space we can provide for the audience, we will operate refunds on a "last purchased, first refunded" basis
