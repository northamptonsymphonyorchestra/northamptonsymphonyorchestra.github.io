2019/20 - A Season of Contrasts

After the whirlwind of our 125th season celebrations, the NSO will be presenting a series of concerts for 2019/20, each with its own flavour - a mix of familiar favourites and hidden treasures.

The online brochure below is a way of finding out more about the the music we will be sharing in the 2019/20 season - click on the image for more details. Soloists will be announced soon, along with ticket links so please come back again for more exciting details and to book your tickets.

19:30 Saturday 12th October 2019 - The Malcolm Arnold Festival

St. Matthew's Church, The Drive, Kingsley, Northampton NN1 4RY

  • Malcolm Arnold Peterloo Overture
  • Sergei Rachmaninoff Piano Concerto No.2 - Featuring Rythmie Wong, Piano
  • Malcolm Arnold Suite from "Stolen Face" - Featuring Rythmie Wong, Piano
  • Malcolm Arnold Variations on a Theme of Ruth Gipps
  • P. I. Tchaikovsky Romeo and Juliet, Fantasy Overture
