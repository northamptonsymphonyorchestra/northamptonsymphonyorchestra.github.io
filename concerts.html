1893 - 2018 - Our 125th Season

2018/19 marks the 125th season for the Northampton Symphony Orchestra. Please join us to celebrate this season with some exciting concerts and incredible soloists.

The online brochure below is a way of finding out more about the the music we will be sharing this season - click on the image for a concert schedule; interesting content about the programmes and soloists; and links to book your tickets for each concert.
